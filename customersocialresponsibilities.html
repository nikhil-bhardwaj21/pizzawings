Corporate Social Responsibilities

Pizza Wings, even though considered a food and pizza delivery company, Its corporate social responsibility is to thank its loyal customers and the community it serves and operates in. Company doing just that by extending a helping hand to local charity. Company has organized "Clean City Green City" programs, Blood donation camps, spread awareness in people about wastage of food and also distributed free food and pizza to helpless people. Pizza wings also putting efforts for awareness about some more social programs like beti bachao beti padhao, wear helmet while driving, use dustbins to keep city clean!
